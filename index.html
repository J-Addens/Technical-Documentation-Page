Prepare Workstation

A tidy place is a productive place!

  1. Remove all items from your desk you don't need
  2. Tidy up your room
  3. Make a To-Do list
  4. Grab all neccesary items
  5. Start your favourite playlist

Make sure you don't skip any steps. Double check if your workstation is ready.

Get Comfortable

Prepare your chair to you standards. When you have to sit for a long time, you want it to be as comfortable as can be.
